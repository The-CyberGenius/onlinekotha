FAQ

Common questions

How do I export my WhatsApp chat?
Open any WhatsApp chat → tap the 3 dots menu → More → Export chat → Choose "Include Media" or "Without Media" → Save the .zip file. Then drag & drop it into Kotha. Done!
Is my chat data safe and private?
100%. Your chats are stored encrypted. We never sell your data, never train AI on your personal conversations, and you can delete everything anytime with one click.
How does the AI learn to talk like my contact?
Kotha's AI reads every message in your uploaded chat — their slang, emojis, humor, tone. It learns their exact writing style and responds just like them. It's not generic AI — it becomes them.
Is Kotha free to use?
Yes! Free forever — unlimited chat imports, beautiful viewer, On This Day memories, and 3 AI chats per day. Pro plan (₹99/mo) with unlimited AI is coming soon.
Which languages does Kotha support?
All of them! Hindi, English, Hinglish, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi — you name it. The AI automatically matches whatever language your contact uses in their chats.
