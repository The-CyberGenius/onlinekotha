How to cancel

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. After cancellation:

  • You retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
  • Your account reverts to Free plan — your imported chats remain accessible.
  • No further charges are made.

To cancel, go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription inside the app, or email us at billing@onlinekotha.com.

How to request a refund

Email billing@onlinekotha.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Razorpay payment ID (found in your email receipt)
  • Reason for refund

Eligible refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Non-refundable situations

  • Account terminated due to Terms of Service violations.
  • Refund requests made after the applicable window.
  • Partial month usage beyond the 7-day window.
